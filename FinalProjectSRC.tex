P(X\textgreater k) \$to compare consistency between players with
different usage levels. Modeling number of attempts to first score using
TS\% as the probability of success \(p\)

number of shot attempt a player need to achieve their first successful
score

step 2: use geometirc distribution to find probability of the 2nd
